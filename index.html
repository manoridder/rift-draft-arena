How it works

1 · Ban phase

You ban 3 champions, your opponent bans 3 back. Remove what counters your plan, or simply what you hate playing against.

2 · Draft

Alternating picks like a real lobby. Every pick offers 3 random champions and you get 2 rerolls for the whole draft. Work with what the Rift gives you.

3 · Items

Pick one item per champion based on the enemy comp. Anti-heal into healers, magic resist into AP, shield-cutting into enchanters.

4 · Analysis

Only after the match do you see tiers, synergy, counters and item verdicts. Copy your result and challenge your friends on the same daily seed.
