SR ITEM IMPORTER

Pulls the live item database straight from Riot's Data Dragon, keeps only what exists on Classic Summoner's Rift 5v5 (map 11), and exports a clean sr-items.json. Queue variants (Arena 22xxxx, Swiftplay 32xxxx, suffix clones like 667666) are folded automatically, Arena-flagged strays are filtered out, and every row has a checkbox so you keep final say over the export. This export becomes the Phase 2 item pool for Rift Draft Arena.
